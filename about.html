About JC Social Media

Welcome to JC Social Media. We’re a digital agency specialising in social media marketing. As you probably guessed.

Here’s more about us, what we do and how we can help you.

What we do

We work with our clients to grow their online influence. In turn, we help their businesses flourish by supporting commercial goals such as sales and lead-generation.

We become our client’s trusted partners in all things digital media. We advise, consult and train or we represent them online. We write in their voice, act on their behalf and join forces with them in growing their company, attracting opportunities or making a difference. If you’re not sure which services you need, we’ll help work out the best option.

We'll manage everything for you

About social media management

Who we work with

We represent companies and individuals who do exceptional work; whether that’s creating exceptional products, providing an exceptional service or sharing an exceptional cause.

The work we do for them helps them reach and serve more people. It puts their products and services in front of relevant audiences with whom they resonate.

There’s no hiding on social media so your brand must be truly worth shouting about! If it is, we’ll help you do just that.

Let's do this!

Our history

JC Social Media began in 2011 when our founder decided she wanted to help make brands’ Facebook and Twitter accounts more effective. From humble beginnings (a spare bedroom, in fact), the business has evolved into the multi-award-winning agency it is today.

Over the years, and a few slightly bigger offices later, the company has represented clients throughout an ever-changing digital landscape, continually learning and setting the standard for effective social media management.

Today

Today the business operates across the UK and the globe. We represent businesses of all sizes as well as train in-house teams in the art of social media. Our team is switched on, commercially aware, and ready to partner with you.

The team is truly multi-talented, with experts in search marketing, graphic design and web development meaning we can look after your entire digital presence. Below, you’ll meet the team making a success of your campaigns.
